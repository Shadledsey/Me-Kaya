What payment options does Me-Kaya support?

Me-Kaya supports a total of 3 payment options:

  • GCash
  • Linked Bank Account
  • cash on delivery

    • Cash on Delivery

    Pay for your orders in cash when they are delivered to your doorstep by our Me-kaya Supported Couriers or go cashless using GCash or other e-waller and banks by scanning the SPX rider's QR PH code upon delivery.

    Online Banking

    Pay at any Me-kaya supported online banking medium.

    GCash

    GCash offers personalized QR codes for easy fund transfers and has integrated features like GLoan and GInsure, providing users with access to financial assistance and insurance services
