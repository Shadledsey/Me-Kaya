HOT Questions

What is the return policy?

We want ME-Kaya customers to be completely satisfied with their purchases! You have 14 days from the delivery date to return unopened and unused items in original packaging for a full refund. Opened items can also be returned for a full refund if they are defective or significantly different from the description we provided. Digital downloads and custom-made items are not eligible or qualified for return. To return an item, contact us for a return authorization number and follow the easy return instructions we will provide. We will process your return and issue a full refund within five business days of receiving it back. For more details, please contact our team through the mobile number or email provided on our page

What are the shipping options available?

We offer a variety of shipping options to fit your needs and budget! Choose standard shipping for cost-effective delivery in 3-7 business days, or upgrade to fast shipping for speedier arrival within 1-3 business days. For the most urgent needs, we offer next-day delivery in select areas. Free shipping is available for orders above a certain amount, or you can choose our flat-rate shipping option for simpler pricing. We also provide additional services like signature confirmation, where the carrier requires someone to sign for it upon delivery for added security, international shipping for global customers, and local pickup for those in our area to save on costs.


When will I receive my order?

Estimated delivery timeframes for each shipping option will be displayed on your product pages and during checkout. However, order processing, carrier delays, and holidays could affect delivery times. We will also provide a user-friendly order tracking system where customers can easily monitor the status of their shipment.

Where are you located?

We are based in Baguio City and are currently only available online.


Can I make changes to an order I already placed?

Orders could be changed as long as our team has not confirmed it
